Dr. Liu Hang is a Research Fellow at Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine, National University of Singapore (NUS). His research has been focused on developing biocompatible scaffolds for 3D cell culture to build disease models and transplants for possible treatments. He is an enthusiastic and highly motivated biomaterial researcher with multi-disciplinary research experience, skilled laboratory techniques, and teaching experience.

Prior to this, he have earned a Ph.D. degree from Faculty of Science, NUS, a M.Sc. degree in Manufacturing Systems & Engineering from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, a B.Eng. degree in Electrical Engineering from Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU) and a B.Eng. degree in Electrical Engineering with First Class Honors from the University of Liverpool (UoL).

Research Interests

Tissue engineering and regenerative medicine, stem cell therapy, scaffold-based 3D cell culture, 3D biomaterials
